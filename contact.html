If you are interested in buying or commissioning a painting or you have any questions regarding my work please get in touch. Some of the paintings in the Gallery are not for sale but most of them are. Prices for small Still Lifes and Landscapes start at £175 unframed.
Portrait commission prices are as follows-

£375 - 30x25cms

£750 - 50x40cms

£1000 upwards for bigger pieces, to be agreed

I try and work from life which means I need 3-5 sittings but where the sitter is short of time I can supplement with photographs.

I am happy to travel to the sitter’s home or workplace or paint from my studio in Fulham.
